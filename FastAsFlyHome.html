Road Flats

Based on Nike's Breaking2 Project in 2016, this line of marathon racing shoes has dominated at all major marathons since its introduction,even being banned

They all feature a high stack height, rockered geometry that promotes an aggresive forefoot strike, and a rigid plate (often carbon fibre) sandwiched between highly reponsive foam.
